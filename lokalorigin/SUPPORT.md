# LokalChoice Support

Welcome to LokalChoice! We're here to help you get the most out of the app.

## Getting Started

### How to Scan a Product
1. Open the app and allow camera access when prompted
2. Point your camera at a product barcode
3. The app will automatically detect and scan the barcode
4. View the product's origin and information

### Alternative Scanning Methods
- **Photo Upload**: Tap the photo icon to select an image from your library containing a barcode
- **Manual Entry**: Tap "Manual Entry" to type in a barcode number directly

## Features

### View Product Origins
After scanning, you'll see:
- Product name and brand
- Country of origin with flag
- Product image (when available)
- Category information

### Find Local Alternatives
- Tap "Find Alternatives" on any product result
- Select your home country
- Browse locally-made alternatives in the same category

### Scan History
- All your scans are saved automatically
- Access your history from the main menu
- Tap any item to view details again
- Swipe to delete items you don't need

### Premium Features
LokalChoice Premium provides unlimited monthly scans. Free users have a limited number of scans per month.

## Troubleshooting

### Camera Issues
**Problem**: Camera won't scan barcodes
- Ensure camera access is enabled in Settings > LokalChoice
- Make sure the barcode is well-lit and clearly visible
- Try cleaning your camera lens
- Ensure the barcode is within the scan frame

**Problem**: Camera is black or not working
- Check that you've granted camera permission
- Restart the app
- Check if other apps can access the camera

### Product Not Found
If a product isn't found in the database:
- Verify the barcode is correct
- Try manual entry to ensure accuracy
- Some products may not be in the OpenFoodFacts database yet

### Photo Upload Issues
**Problem**: Barcode not detected in photo
- Ensure the barcode is clearly visible and in focus
- Try taking a new photo with better lighting
- Make sure the entire barcode is in the frame

### Scan Line Frozen
If the green scan line stops moving:
- Dismiss any error messages by tapping the X
- The scan line should resume automatically
- If not, try closing and reopening the app

### Subscription Issues
**Problem**: Purchase didn't activate
- Check your internet connection
- Restart the app to allow purchase verification
- Check Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions to verify the purchase
- Contact us if the issue persists

**Problem**: Can't restore purchase
- Ensure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for purchase
- Tap "Restore Purchases" in the subscription modal
- Wait for verification to complete

## Privacy & Data

LokalChoice is designed with privacy in mind:
- All scan history is stored locally on your device
- We don't collect or store personal information
- Product data comes from the public OpenFoodFacts database
- No tracking or analytics

## Technical Requirements

- iOS 15.0 or later
- iPhone or iPad
- Camera access (for barcode scanning)
- Photo library access (optional, for photo upload)
- Internet connection (for product lookups)

## Data Sources

LokalChoice uses product data from [OpenFoodFacts](https://world.openfoodfacts.org), a free and open database of food products. Product information accuracy depends on community contributions to this database.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is LokalChoice free?**
A: Yes, LokalChoice is free with a limited number of scans per month. Premium subscription provides unlimited scans.

**Q: Do you collect my data?**
A: No. All data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect or track personal information.

**Q: Why isn't my product in the database?**
A: We use OpenFoodFacts, a community-driven database. Not all products are included yet.

**Q: Can I use this offline?**
A: The camera and scanning work offline, but you need an internet connection to look up product information.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
A: Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > LokalChoice and select Cancel Subscription.

**Q: What countries are supported?**
A: LokalChoice works worldwide and includes data for products from many countries. Product availability depends on the OpenFoodFacts database.
